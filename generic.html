About Me

I live at the intersection of data, people, and purpose. I’m here for impact, not just output

Elizabeth Osanyinro is a data scientist and business analyst passionate about NLP, ethical AI, and inclusive innovation. She holds an MSc in Applied Artificial Intelligence and Data Analytics from the University of Bradford, where she graduated with distinction. At CarbonNote AI, she applies machine learning to support climate-conscious decisions, turning complex data into human-centered solutions. She is skilled in Python, SQL, and Power BI. Her expertise spans sentiment analysis, topic modeling, fraud detection, and retrieval-augmented generation (RAG). She is also a co-creator of COPGPT — a FastAPI-powered, hybrid search chatbot that delivers intelligent sustainability recommendations.

Outside of her core work, Elizabeth is the founder and co-organiser of PyData Bradford, a growing community that empowers data professionals and newcomers through open learning and collaboration. She has spoken at PyData London and PyData Manchester, sharing insights on responsible AI, NLP, and community-driven tech. She has co-authored academic papers on COPGPT — a RAG-based policy recommendation chatbot, and the role of AI in transforming modern newsrooms. Whether through research, code, or community, Elizabeth is committed to using data for impact, not just output.
